What We Offer

Capabilities that take biodevices from idea to working system

The BioDevice Foundry combines cleanroom microfabrication with rapid prototyping, biofunctionalization, and device/material characterization — all in one place.

If you’re new, start with “Access & Training” to understand onboarding. If you already have access, book through NEMO.

How to use this page

Most projects use more than one area. For example, a microfluidic device might involve lithography and etching (fabrication), PDMS molding and bonding (biofunctionalization), laser cutting for housings (prototyping), and microscopy for inspection (characterization).

If you don’t know which process you need, that’s normal — contact us and we’ll help you route to the right pathway.
