Thin Film Deposition

Thin film deposition enables functional layers used in biodevices—such as electrodes, adhesion layers, protective coatings, and device stack components.

Share your target materials and thickness ranges and we’ll help determine feasibility and process flow.

Contact for guidance Back to What We Offer Access & Training

What this enables

  • Electrodes and conductive layers for sensing/actuation
  • Adhesion and barrier layers within device stacks
  • Thin coatings to support bonding, passivation, or surface behavior
  • Repeatable films for prototyping and small runs

Typical inputs / outputs

  • Inputs: substrate type/size, target material(s), thickness, uniformity requirements
  • Outputs: deposited film ready for patterning, etching, or downstream processing

Requirements

  • Training + safety requirements
  • Compatibility checks for materials and substrates
  • Booking (self-serve) or staff-assisted runs
