About Biodevice Foundry

Established in 2015, the Biodevice Foundry is a leading biomedical research facility at UBC. We provide researchers with the tools, expertise, and collaborative environment needed to transform innovative ideas into practical medical devices.

Our interdisciplinary team brings together engineers, scientists, and clinicians to support projects across multiple domains including diagnostics, therapeutics, and medical imaging.
