Wet Chemical Processing

Wet chemical processing supports cleaning, etching, and controlled chemical workflows used in device fabrication and surface preparation steps.

Because wet processing is highly material- and chemistry-dependent, we recommend contacting staff early to confirm compatibility.

  • Controlled chemical preparation steps that support repeatability
  • Surface preparation and readiness for subsequent bonding or chemistry
