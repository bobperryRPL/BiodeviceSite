How to find us (basement level)

The Foundry is located in the SBME building on the basement level, room B1130.

  • Enter the SBME building at 6088 University Blvd.
  • Go down to the basement level (stairs or elevator).
  • Look for signage for the BioDevice Foundry and follow directions to B1130.
  • If you can’t find it, send us a message and we’ll guide you.
